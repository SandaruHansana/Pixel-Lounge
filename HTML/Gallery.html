Top Movies and Top Games Of The Year
2020 - 2023

2020 - Hamilton

Director: Thomas Kail
The real life of one of America's foremost founding fathers and first Secretary of the Treasury, Alexander Hamilton. Captured live on Broadway from the Richard Rodgers Theater with the original Broadway cast.

2021 - Spider-Man: No Way Home

Director: Jon Watts
With Spider-Man's identity now revealed, Peter asks Doctor Strange for help. When a spell goes wrong, dangerous foes from other worlds start to appear, forcing Peter to discover what it truly means to be Spider-Man.

2022 - Top Gun: Maverick

Director: Joseph Kosinski
After thirty years, Maverick is still pushing the envelope as a top naval aviator, but must confront ghosts of his past when he leads TOP GUN's elite graduates on a mission that demands the ultimate sacrifice from those chosen to fly it.

2023 - Transformers: Rise of the Beasts

Director: Steven Caple Jr.
Plot unknown. Reportedly based on the 'Transformers' spinoff 'Beast Wars' which feature robots that transform into robotic animals.

2020 - Teardown

Developer: Tuxedo Labs
Format: PC (Steam early access)
A first-person heist game where you play with physics as a one-person demolitions team instead of a stealthy cat burglar.

2021 -Far Cry 6

Developer: Ubisoft Toronto
Format: PC, PS5, PS4, Xbox One, Xbox Series X
An open world chaos simulator where you shoot, drive and fly across an island to free the bits you don't destroy.

2022 - God of War Ragnarok

Developer: Sony Santa Monica Studio
Platform(s): PS5, PS4
The highly-anticipated sequel to the 2018 God of War reboot, continuing the journey of Kratos and Atreus.

2023 - Counter-Strike 2

Platform(s): PC
Value officially announced that a follow-up to its hit FPS is on the way, and that it will be available as a free upgrade to CS:GO. Set to introduce a variety of new and improve features, Counter-Strike 2 will include smoke grenades that interact with the environment and react to elements such as lighting, explosions, and gunfire.
